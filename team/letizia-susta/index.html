Letizia Susta

Project Assistant

Letizia supports clients through various communications, research and analysis tasks in the ICT sector, with a focus on cybersecurity, data protection and telecommunications.

Letizia has been working in European affairs since 2015, joining Nove in April 2017.

Before joining the Nove team, Letizia completed traineeships at the EU Delegation of the Italian Federation of the Chemical Industry (Federchimica) and at the General Confederation of Italian Industry (Confindustria) in Romania, where she worked in the fields of health, energy and business services. Prior to that, she completed a traineeship at the European Parliament, focusing on foreign affairs, security and development. Her diverse professional experience also includes work in public relations as an external collaborator at the European University Institute in Florence, Italy, and as an intern for the commercial department of a consultancy firm in Shanghai, China.
